## Simple Interest Calculator

A simple interest calculator computes the interest earned or paid on a principal amount over a specified time period using a fixed interest rate.

The formula is:

    Simple Interest = Principal × Rate × Time

Where:
- `Principal` is the initial amount of money.
- `Rate` is the annual interest rate (in decimal form).
- `Time` is the time the money is invested or borrowed for, in years.

Example:
- Principal: $1000
- Rate: 5% (0.05)
- Time: 3 years

Calculated simple interest:

    1000 × 0.05 × 3 = 150

The total amount after 3 years would be:

    Principal + Simple Interest = 1000 + 150 = 1150
